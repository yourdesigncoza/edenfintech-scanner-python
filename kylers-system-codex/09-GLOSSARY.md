# Glossary (Condensed)

- **Base Case**: Most likely operating outcome used in valuation.
- **Catalyst**: Event/process likely to change fundamentals or perception.
- **CAGR**: Annualized compounded return from current price to target.
- **Circle of Competence**: Industries/situations you can actually underwrite.
- **Dilution**: Share count growth that reduces per-share ownership.
- **Downside Case**: Reasonable adverse outcome from current price.
- **FCF Margin**: Free cash flow divided by revenue.
- **Forward Return**: Expected annualized return from today's price.
- **Hurdle Rate**: Minimum acceptable expected CAGR for new capital.
- **Moat**: Durable advantage difficult for competitors to replicate.
- **Normalized Margin**: Mid-cycle margin estimate used to avoid trough/peak distortion.
- **Position Size**: Portfolio percentage allocated to one position.
- **ROIC/ROCE**: Capital efficiency metrics to assess business quality.
- **Solvency**: Ability to meet obligations and survive until turnaround matures.
- **Theme Correlation**: Shared catalyst/risk drivers across holdings.
