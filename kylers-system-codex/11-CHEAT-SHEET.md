# Codex Cheat Sheet

## One-Line Strategy
Buy quality businesses in temporary distress, only when downside is bounded and catalysts are visible.

## Process
1. Find underloved themes with catalysts.
2. Run 5 first filters: solvency, dilution, growth path, capital efficiency, valuation feasibility.
3. Compare peers and keep highest quality/asymmetry mix.
4. Deep-dive moat, management, incentives, and catalyst stack.
5. Value with simple model: revenue x FCF margin x multiple, plus debt/share adjustments.
6. Score opportunity: downside, probability, CAGR.
7. Size from asymmetry with hard breakpoints.
8. Monitor thesis integrity quarterly and recycle capital by forward returns.

## Non-Negotiables
- No catalyst -> no position
- No survival runway -> no position
- <30% expected CAGR -> no new capital (except documented long-run exception)
- Probability <60% -> no new capital
- Respect concentration and correlation limits

## Sell Triggers
- Forward returns compressed below required levels
- Better opportunity with superior asymmetry
- Thesis broken by facts

## Meta Rule
Consistency beats precision. Subjective estimates are acceptable; undisciplined process is not.
