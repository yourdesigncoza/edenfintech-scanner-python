# Step 8: After the Buy

Owning well is where edge compounds.

## Monitoring Questions

- Are expected catalysts arriving on time?
- Is management behavior aligned with commitments?
- Are margins/revenue tracking thesis ranges?
- Is competitor position improving against us?
- Which macro events truly change cash-flow trajectory?

## Sell Rules

Sell or trim when:
1. Target reached and forward returns no longer clear hurdle
2. Sharp rerating compresses forward CAGR to low-teens zone
3. Facts break thesis (not price action alone)

## Behavior Rule

Use information to manage emotion:
- Re-underwrite before reacting
- Separate volatility from thesis break

## Codex Extrapolation

Maintain a standing `Thesis Integrity Checklist` each quarter:
- What improved
- What degraded
- What is unchanged
- What invalidates position now

No checklist update = no add decision.
