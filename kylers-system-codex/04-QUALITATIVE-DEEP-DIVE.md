# Step 4: Qualitative Deep Dive

Numbers explain outcomes. Qualitative work explains persistence.

## Five Required Questions

1. Moat / durable advantage:
- Low cost, switching cost, regulation, brand, capital barrier, network effect

2. Management quality:
- Operating history in similar situations
- Behavior under stress
- Execution vs narrative consistency

3. Problem/fix map:
- What broke?
- What exact actions are being taken?
- What evidence shows traction?

4. Incentive alignment:
- Pay tied to what: revenue, EPS, EBITDA, FCF, ROIC?
- Is gameable metric risk high?

5. Catalyst stack:
- What changes business fundamentals or market perception?
- When does each catalyst hit?

## Hard Rule

No catalyst stack -> no position.

## Codex Extrapolation

Classify each catalyst by confidence:
- `Hard`: contractual/regulatory/signed and dated
- `Medium`: management-led with measurable milestones
- `Soft`: macro narrative or sentiment rerating

Base case must not depend on Soft catalysts alone.
