# Step 2: First Filter

Every candidate must pass all 5 checks.

## 1) Solvency
Question: Can this business survive long enough for the thesis to play out?

Check:
- Cash + liquidity runway
- Debt maturity wall timing
- FCF generation trend
- Refinancing feasibility

Fail condition:
- Survival uncertain and risk is not already priced in.

## 2) Dilution
Question: Is capital allocation shareholder-aligned on a per-share basis?

Check:
- Share count trend
- SBC intensity vs actual growth
- Equity issuance purpose (value creation vs survival funding)

Fail condition:
- Persistent dilution without per-share value creation.

## 3) Revenue Path
Question: Can revenue grow (or normalize) in the modeled horizon?

Check:
- Historical cycle behavior
- Demand drivers and timing
- Segment-level growth realism

Fail condition:
- No credible growth/normalization route.

## 4) Capital Efficiency
Question: Is capital use acceptable for the business type?

Check:
- Long-run ROIC/ROCE (with cyclical context)
- Relative position vs peers

Fail condition:
- Structurally weak returns with no evidence of turnaround.

## 5) Valuation Feasibility
Question: Can this plausibly clear hurdle returns?

Check:
- P/S, P/FCF, EV/FCF, EV/EBITDA vs own history
- Normalized margin sanity
- Preliminary CAGR from current price

Fail condition:
- Cannot reasonably reach required return.

## Codex Extrapolation: Fast Rejects

Immediate reject unless disproven:
- Equity issuance to pay debt service
- Multi-year margin decay with no operational fix
- Catalyst narrative based only on macro hope
