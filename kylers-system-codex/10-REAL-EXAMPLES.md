# Pattern Library from Wiki Cases

This file captures repeatable setups seen across CPS, BABA, BA, HRL, OMI, TCMD, QXO, and others.

## Setup A: Solvency Scare but Survival Secured

Traits:
- Market prices near-distress
- Debt/liquidity fix emerges (refi, maturities pushed, asset sales)
- Operating costs structurally reduced

Key test:
- Is survival now probable before catalyst horizon?

## Setup B: Quality Franchise, Temporary Margin Compression

Traits:
- Demand durability intact
- Input-cost or execution shock compresses margins
- Price reflects permanence while evidence suggests normalization

Key test:
- Is margin damage structural or timing-based?

## Setup C: Strong Business, Narrative Discount

Traits:
- Fundamentals stable/improving
- Macro/political/geographic narrative drives discount
- Balance sheet enables patient execution

Key test:
- Does expected return remain strong after applying permanent risk discount?

## Setup D: New Operator, Old Asset

Traits:
- Credible CEO/operator with relevant prior track record
- Early operational milestones met
- Culture/process changes visible before full financial recovery

Key test:
- Is execution quality already changing slope, not just rhetoric?

## Anti-Patterns

- Cheap with no catalyst timeline
- High upside claim with fragile financing
- Thesis depending on multiple expansion only
- Position sizing based on confidence language, not downside math
